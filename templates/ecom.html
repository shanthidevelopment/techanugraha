E-Commerce Business Models and Strategies

In the ever-growing world of e-commerce, understanding various business models is crucial for success. This course dives into the strategies used by top online businesses, from B2B to B2C, and subscription-based models to marketplaces. You will learn how to identify profitable niches, create value propositions, and implement marketing, sales, and customer acquisition strategies. By the end, you'll be equipped with the knowledge to build your own online store or scale an existing business.
